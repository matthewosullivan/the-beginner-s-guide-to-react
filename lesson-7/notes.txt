One important thing to note about the style prop is that the 
values are not vendor prefixed for you, so you'll have to do that yourself.

There are a couple other problems within in-line styles as well. 
There are a couple libraries to overcome some of these problems. 
Popular libraries include styled components, emotion, and glamorous. 

https://www.styled-components.com/
https://emotion.sh/
https://glamorous.rocks/
